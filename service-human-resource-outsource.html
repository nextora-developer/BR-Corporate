SME HR Compliance Checklist

Check your current standing against Malaysian HR standards.

A) Employment Documentation
  • Signed contract/offer letter for every employee
  • Clear probation & confirmation terms
  • Working hours, leave, & salary terms documented
B) Payroll & Records
  • Payslips issued & payroll records maintained
  • Leave & attendance tracking in place
  • Claims/allowances recorded properly
C) Statutory Readiness
  • EPF / SOCSO / EIS registration readiness
  • Monthly reminders for compliance items
D) HR Governance
  • Policies clearly communicated to staff
  • Disciplinary & resignation steps documented
