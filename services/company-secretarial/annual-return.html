Annual Compliance Checklist (Sdn. Bhd.)

Use this as a simple guide to stay compliant:

Annual Return (SSM)

  • Annual Return (AR) planned and submitted on time
  • Company particulars reviewed before filing (directors, address, shareholding)

Statutory Records

  • Statutory registers updated and properly organised
  • Resolutions and minutes recorded where applicable

Corporate Changes

  • Director changes lodged (if any)
  • Shareholding updates lodged (if any)
  • Registered office / company details updated (if applicable)

Practical Readiness

  • Company information consistent for banking, vendor and audit needs
  • Compliance file maintained for future reference

If you’re unsure whether your records are up to date, we can advise the correct sequence before filing.

What’s Not Included (Out of Scope)

To keep scope clear and service delivery smooth, the following items are not included unless stated otherwise.

Disputes, litigation, demand letters and any form of legal representation are not included under standard Annual Return and statutory filing support.

M&A, group restructuring, capital reduction, strike-off or liquidation are not part of standard AR filing services and require a separate service scope.

Historical non-compliance or multiple years of outstanding filings may require a compliance recovery plan and additional quotation.

Drafting shareholders’ agreements, JV agreements or investment agreements is not included unless expressly stated.

Banking or financing negotiations, loan facilities and approval outcomes are not included. Documentation support may be provided where applicable.

Delays caused by SSM processing, banks, authorities, or late client response are outside our control and are not included in turnaround estimates.

If your case requires additional work (e.g. outdated or incomplete records), we will advise first and provide a separate quotation before proceeding.
