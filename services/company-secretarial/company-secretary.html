Company Compliance Checklist (Sdn. Bhd.)

Here are the key compliance areas we help you manage:

Mandatory Appointments & Records

  • Maintain appointed Company Secretary at all times
  • Maintain registered office & statutory record location
  • Keep Register of Directors / Members / Shareholdings updated
  • Maintain resolutions, minutes and statutory documents properly

Statutory Filings & Annual Obligations

  • Annual Return (AR) filing with SSM (on time)
  • Lodge updates with SSM for corporate changes (as required)
  • Keep company records organised for audit / banking readiness

Common Changes Requiring Documentation

  • Director appointment / resignation / change of particulars
  • Shareholding changes (transfer / issuance where applicable)
  • Change of registered office / business address
  • Bank signatory approvals / board resolutions

Good Governance Practices (Recommended)

  • Clear approval process for major decisions
  • Updated compliance file for audit readiness
  • Records matching operational reality (signatories, ownership)

Some obligations vary depending on your company’s circumstances. We will advise based on your case.

What’s Not Included (Out of Scope)

Clear scope boundaries help us deliver faster and more consistently.

Disputes, litigation, demand letters and any form of legal representation are not included under standard CoSec support.

M&A, group restructuring, capital reduction, strike-off or liquidation require a separate service scope and quotation.

Share valuation, tax structuring and stamp duty advisory are specialist services and will be quoted separately if required.

Loan facility negotiations, financing approvals and outcome-dependent banking matters are not included. We can support documentation where applicable.

Shareholders agreements, JV agreements and investment agreements are not included unless stated otherwise.

Non-compliance recovery may require a separate compliance recovery plan. Delays caused by third parties (SSM/banks) or late client response are not included.

If your request is outside standard scope, we will advise first and provide a separate quotation before proceeding.
