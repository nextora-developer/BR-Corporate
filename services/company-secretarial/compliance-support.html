What’s Not Included (Out of Scope)

To keep scope clear and service delivery smooth, the following are not included unless stated otherwise:

Legal advice, disputes, litigation, demand letters and any form of legal representation are not included under this service.

Mergers, acquisitions, capital restructuring or group restructuring exercises are not included and require a separate scope of work.

Drafting shareholders’ agreements, JV agreements, investment agreements or other complex legal contracts are outside the scope of this service.

Tax advisory, planning and tax filings are handled separately under accounting / tax service scope and are not included here.

Company strike-off, winding-up or liquidation processes are separate service scopes and will be quoted independently.

Negotiations related to banking facilities, financing approvals or loan arrangements are not included in this service scope.

Recovery of long-term non-compliance (e.g. multiple years of missing records or filings) may require a separate compliance recovery plan and quotation.

Delays arising from third parties (authorities, banks, vendors) or late client response are outside our control and not part of the service scope.
