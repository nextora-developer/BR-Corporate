Director / Shareholder Changes

Proper documentation and statutory lodgement for company changes — accurate, compliant, and audit-ready.

Changes in directors, shareholders, or company particulars must be recorded correctly and lodged where required to keep your Sdn. Bhd. compliant and in good standing with SSM.

It’s best to update changes promptly to avoid inconsistencies in statutory records.

What This Service Covers

Key director, shareholder and company changes documented and lodged correctly.

Director appointment / resignation / change of particulars

Shareholder / shareholding updates (subject to scope & documentation)

Change of registered office / company address (where applicable)

Preparation of required resolutions, minutes & statutory documents

Lodgement / submission coordination with relevant authorities (where required)

Guidance on document requirements, sequence & compliance steps

Why Director / Shareholder Updates Matter

Accurate & Compliant Records

Keeps company records accurate and compliant under SSM requirements.

Reduced Risk for Banking & Audits

Reduces risk of issues during banking, audits, tenders and due diligence.

Proper Approvals & Authority

Ensures approvals and signatory authority are properly documented and traceable.

Avoid Compliance Clean-Up Costs

Avoids future “compliance clean-up” costs caused by outdated or inconsistent records.

Common Change Requests We Handle

Director-related, shareholder-related and company particulars changes.

Director-related

  • Appointment of new director
  • Resignation / removal of director
  • Update of director particulars (address / details)

Shareholder / Ownership-related

  • Share transfer between shareholders (subject to supporting documents)
  • Updates to shareholding structure (subject to scope)
  • Internal ownership changes requiring proper resolutions

Company Particulars

  • Change of registered office / address
  • Updates to company information where required

If multiple changes are involved, we’ll advise the correct sequence to avoid rework and delays.

Changes & Record-Keeping Checklist (Sdn. Bhd.)

Key areas to ensure your director, shareholder and company changes are properly recorded.

Director Changes

  • Appointment / resignation properly documented (resolutions / minutes)
  • Statutory register updated with latest director details
  • Filing / lodgement completed where required
  • Signatory authority updated for records and banks (where relevant)

Shareholding Changes

  • Supporting documents prepared and signed (where applicable)
  • Shareholding registers updated
  • Required statutory documentation completed
  • Ownership records consistent for audit / due diligence readiness

Company Particulars

  • Registered office / address updates properly recorded
  • Company records reflect latest operational reality
  • Compliance file maintained (resolutions, confirmations, submissions)

If you’re unsure whether previous changes were properly lodged, we can perform a quick record review.

What’s Not Included (Out of Scope)

Clear scope boundaries help us deliver faster and more consistently.

Legal advice, disputes, litigation, demand letters and any form of legal representation are not included under this service.

Complex restructuring (M&A, group restructuring, capital reduction) and related exercises are outside standard scope and will require a separate proposal.

Share valuation, tax structuring and stamp duty advisory are specialist services and are not included unless separately engaged.

Drafting complex agreements (shareholders / JV / investment agreements and similar) is not part of standard scope unless specifically stated.

Strike-off, liquidation and long-term non-compliance clean-up are separate services. These may require a structured compliance recovery plan and separate quotation.

Banking / financing negotiations (loan facilities, approvals, negotiations) are not included. Delays caused by SSM, banks or late client responses are also outside our control.

If your request falls outside standard scope, we will advise first and provide a separate quotation before proceeding.
